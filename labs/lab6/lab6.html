10/14/2021
Adrianna Singh

For this lab, we were required to create a logo and banner to create a presentable homepage. We used code.org to make a black and white and a colored logo using 32 by 32 pixels. These are called a favicon for our html document since they are the icons that sit at the corner of a tab and identify my documents. For the black and white logo I used 1 to make a pixel white and 0 to make a pixel black. For the colored logo I used a combination of 6 digits for each pixel so to make a pixel black I used 000000 and to make a pixel white I used 111111. I also used 111110 for yellow and 111011 for pink. We created the banner using photopea and inserting our favicon.
